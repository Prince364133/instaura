FAQs

What’s the difference between Back-End Support and a Full Mandate?

Back-End gives you architecture and advisory while you lead investor calls. A Full Mandate means we run the raise end to end; you step in for critical moments.

Do you guarantee funds?

No one ethical can. We guarantee architecture and conviction. Capital follows belief and fit.

We’ve paid retainers before with no outcome. Do you still charge retainers?

We use a calibrated mix of retainers + success fees aligned to clear milestones. No open-ended retainers. If the signal isn’t strong enough, we won’t engage.

We’re cash constrained. Can we start without large upfronts?

Start with Model 1 (Back-End Support) or a Signal Call. It creates immediate clarity and momentum without heavy fixed spend.

Can we do success fee only?

We avoid pure success-fee models; they misalign incentives. In select invite-only cases, we may structure reduced retainers with milestone-based success fees.

Will you introduce us to your network?

Yes — when the signal is ready and the fit is right. Intros are sequenced to preserve urgency and terms.

Do you prepare data rooms and documents?

We architect the narrative spine and prepare/advise on all investor materials. Lega
