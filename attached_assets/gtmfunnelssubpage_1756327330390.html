FAQs

Do you guarantee results?

Minimum guarantees are offered only on GTM Execution Ownership Mandates (invite-only). Deliverables/KPIs are mutually agreed upfront (e.g., revenue, client acquisition, conversion benchmarks). If not delivered, fees are refunded or adjusted. Design, Back-End Support, and Strategy models don’t carry guarantees because you control execution.

How are fees structured?
  • Design Models — Fixed fee (typically split 50/50, start + delivery)
  • Execution Back-End Support — Monthly/quarterly retainer
  • Execution Ownership — Retainer + performance-linked component (with minimum guarantees)
  • Funnel Build & Monitoring — Fixed build fee + ongoing retainer
Do you run ads or outbound?

No. We are not an agency. We design the signal-led GTM, funnels, copy, and motions. Your team or partner agencies execute campaigns. In Execution Ownership, we may manage partners/vendors.

What does Funnel Monitoring include?

Monthly recalibration of copy, flows, offers, and handoffs. Leak diagnostics, A/B cadence, and a KPI review across CAC, CVR, and LTV with corrective actions.

Can we start with just a diagnostic?

Yes. Most teams begin with a Signal Funnel Diagnostic or Signal Call to establish readiness and the first three moves.

How long to install a funnel?

Typically 3–5 weeks for strategy, copy, build, integrations, QA, and go-live.

Which tools and CRMs do you support?

Systeme.io, GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, plus common payment gateways and analytics stacks — all implemented with signal-first design.

Is this only for startups?

No. We serve startups, SMEs, and enterprises. Signal scales across all.

How do we qualify for Execution Ownership?

Yas will qualify first. We take limited ownership mandates where belief-fit is clear and the motion is ready for scale in SEA, Middle East, or US.
